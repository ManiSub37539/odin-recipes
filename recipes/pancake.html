Recipe to make an Apple Pancake!

home

Description

Apple pancake is a delicious and easy-to-make breakfast dish that is perfect for any occasion. This recipe combines the sweetness of apples with the light and fluffy texture of pancakes to create a dish that is sure to impress. Whether you are cooking for a crowd or just want to enjoy a cozy breakfast at home, apple pancake is the perfect choice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 cup chopped apples

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. In a medium bowl, beat eggs with milk. Stir in flour and salt until just moistened. Do not overmix.
  3. Place butter in a 10-inch ovenproof skillet and melt in the preheated oven. Remove skillet from oven and pour in the batter. Return skillet to oven and bake for 20 minutes.
  4. While pancake is baking, combine white sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a small bowl. Stir in walnuts and apples.
  5. After 20 minutes, remove pancake from oven and sprinkle sugar mixture over the top. Return pancake to oven and bake for an additional 5 minutes, or until pancake is puffed and golden brown.
  6. Serve immediately.
