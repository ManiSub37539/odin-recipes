Recipe to make Steak!

home

Description

Steak is a classic dish that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you are cooking for a crowd or just want to enjoy a cozy dinner at home, steak is the perfect choice. This recipe is easy to make and can be customized to suit your tastes. Whether you like your steak rare, medium-rare, or well-done, this recipe will help you achieve the perfect steak every time.

Ingredients:

  • 2 (1-inch thick) boneless rib-eye or New York strip steaks
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Remove steaks from the refrigerator and let them sit at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  3. Season steaks generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large ovenproof skillet over medium-high heat. Add steaks and sear until browned, about 3 minutes per side.
  5. Add butter, garlic, and rosemary to the skillet. Baste steaks with butter mixture for 1 minute.
  6. Transfer skillet to the preheated oven and cook steaks to desired doneness, about 5-7 minutes for medium-rare.
  7. Remove steaks from the oven and let them rest for 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
